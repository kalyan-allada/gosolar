Kalyan Allada, PhD





Kalyan Allada received his PhD in experimental particle/nuclear physics from University of Kentucky. He then spent several years working as a postdoctoral research scientist at MIT's Laboratory of Nuclear Science and at Jefferson Lab (Department of Energy's laboratory for nuclear science). He enjoys working with large data sets and always eager to learn skills. In his spare time he likes to hike and play tennis.
